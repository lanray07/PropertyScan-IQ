# Privacy Policy for PropertyScan IQ

Effective date: May 17, 2026

PropertyScan IQ is a property inspection and reporting app for creating property profiles, inspections, photo evidence, room notes, AI-assisted visual findings, and PDF reports.

This policy explains what information the app may process and how it is used. It is provided for transparency and should be reviewed before production release.

## Information You Provide

PropertyScan IQ may process information you enter or choose to add to the app, including:

- Property names and addresses
- Owner, client, or business contact details you enter
- Room and inspection notes
- Property photos selected from your photo library or captured with the camera
- Detected issue records, severity labels, categories, and suggested actions
- Generated report text and PDF reports
- Business profile and branding information, such as a company name, footer text, or logo

## How Information Is Used

The app uses this information to:

- Create and manage property inspection records
- Store local inspection history
- Generate room-by-room findings
- Produce report previews and PDF reports
- Enable sharing or exporting reports when you choose to do so
- Support subscription-related feature access

## Local Storage

The current app stores inspection data locally on your device using Apple platform storage technologies. This includes properties, inspections, rooms, photos, issue records, reports, and app settings.

You can delete local app data from within the app settings, or by deleting the app from your device.

## Photos and Camera

PropertyScan IQ requests access to the camera only when you choose to take inspection photos. The app requests photo library access only when you choose to upload photos.

Photos are used to create inspection evidence and support AI-assisted visual issue suggestions. PropertyScan IQ does not access photos unless you choose them or capture them in the app.

## AI Processing

PropertyScan IQ includes AI-assisted visual inspection features. AI findings are suggestions only and must be reviewed by the user.

The current development build uses mock AI mode by default. In a production configuration, selected photos, room notes, inspection type, property type, and related context may be sent to a secure backend endpoint for AI processing. API keys must not be stored in the app.

AI findings are not a certified property survey, legal advice, electrical certification, gas safety certification, or structural engineering advice. Urgent safety concerns should be checked by qualified professionals.

## Subscriptions and Purchases

PropertyScan IQ may offer auto-renewable subscriptions using Apple StoreKit. Purchases and billing are processed by Apple. The app may receive subscription status information from Apple to unlock paid features.

## Sharing and Exporting

You control when reports are exported or shared. When you use iOS sharing features, the destination app or service you choose may process the exported report according to its own privacy policy.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use:

- Apple services for camera, photo picker, local storage, sharing, and StoreKit subscriptions
- A secure backend service for AI processing when remote AI mode is enabled

PropertyScan IQ does not sell personal information.

## Data Retention

Local inspection data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, delete individual records where supported, or remove the app.

If a production backend is enabled, backend retention periods should be documented before release and reflected in this policy.

## Children

PropertyScan IQ is intended for property, inspection, and business workflows. It is not directed to children.
